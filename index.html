We service our clients with eco-friendly cleaning products that work great and leave your home looking good and smelling fresh.

Services

Balancing work, family, and demanding obligations at times can be difficult to achieve. It is our desire to restore your peace of mind and help you recover hours of your valuable time by providing an effective, professional and thorough cleaning service for your home.

Kitchen

  • Wash floors (by hand per request)
  • Clean inside and outside of microwave
  • Clean outside and on top of refrigerator
  • Clean on top and outside of stove and dishwasher
  • Clean outside of cupboard doors and drawers
  • Wipe down all counter tops and scrub inside of sinks
  • Shine faucets
  • Lightly dust blinds
  • Look for cobwebs
  • Empty all trash

Living Room/Bedroom

  • Vacuum all carpets or mop
  • Dust cabinets, dresser, TV’s, picture frames and nick nacks and bedroom frame
  • We pick things up and clean underneath them (not around them)
  • Make beds
  • Change linen (by request)
  • Empty trash
  • Look for cobwebs
  • Lightly dust blinds
  • Wipe window sills
  • Water plants (by request)

Bathrooms

  • Clean toilets, bathtub, bathtub walls & floors
  • Clean sinks, wipe window sills
  • Shine faucets, mirrors, soap dishes & toothbrush holders
  • Wipe all drawers and cabinets and look for finger prints on doors

Premium Cleaning

  • Clean out refrigerators
  • Clean inside of ovens
  • Put clean dishes away
  • Dust blinds on both sides
  • Wet wipe all baseboards
  • Dust inside of china cabinets
  • Cleaning heaters

Window Washing

  • Event cleanings
  • Real estate agent services

Commercial Cleaning Services

  • Vacuum and mop all floors
  • Lightly dust surfaces
  • Sanitize all bathrooms
  • Stocking, soap and paper towels
  • Trash removal
  • Ordering supplies (toilet paper, paper towels, trash bags, soap and Kleenex)
